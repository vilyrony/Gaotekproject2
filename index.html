Advanced Ambient Air Quality Monitoring for Compliance, Clarity, and Control

Accurate ambient air quality monitoring is essential for identifying pollution trends, supporting compliance efforts, and enabling proactive environmental management. Real-time data on key pollutants such as PM2.5, PM10, NOx, SO₂, CO, and O₃ allows industries, municipalities, and environmental agencies to make informed decisions that protect public health and meet regulatory standards.

Built for reliability and long-term deployment, these systems integrate cloud-based connectivity and advanced sensors to deliver high-resolution environmental insights. Designed for B2B applications, these solutions help organizations across North America streamline emissions reporting and improve operational transparency.

Enviro Test Solutions supports this mission through innovative technologies, tailored implementation strategies, and close collaboration with partners like GAO Tek and GAO RFID. From system integration to post-deployment support, the company ensures every client receives expert guidance and scalable solutions aligned with their regulatory and operational needs. Enviro Test Solutions continues to advance environmental testing with a focus on performance, usability, and data integrity.

Core System Components

Hardware Solutions

  • High-Precision Sensors: Utilized with Chemical and Gas Sensors for accurate detection of particulate matter and harmful gases in ambient air environments.
  • Weatherproof Enclosures: Best integrated with Environmental & Agriculture Sensors to ensure robust outdoor deployment and continuous environmental data capture.
  • Telemetry Modules: Enabled through NB-IoT End Devices for efficient long-range, low-power data communication in air quality networks.
  • Solar Power Units (optional): Supported by Solar Power solutions to facilitate energy autonomy in remote ambient monitoring installations.

Software & Cloud Platform

  • User-Friendly Dashboard: Intuitive visualization tools with live air quality index updates, alerts, and data analytics.
  • Custom Alert Triggers: Define thresholds and receive instant notifications via SMS/email for proactive risk management.
  • Data Logging & Export: Easy access to historical records in standard formats for in-depth analysis and regulatory reporting.
  • Over-the-Air Updates: Ensures long-term scalability and security with remote software maintenance.

Success Stories & Case Studies

City of Los Angeles: Urban Air Quality Network

The City of Los Angeles partnered with Enviro Test Solutions to deploy over 100 ambient air quality sensors across residential and industrial zones. The real-time data helped city officials reduce pollution hotspots and enforce zoning laws. The integration with the city’s smart infrastructure platform enabled predictive analytics for air quality forecasting.

Industrial Compliance in Houston, TX

A major petrochemical company used our systems to maintain compliance with EPA emission regulations. Our automated alerts and remote calibration helped reduce manual inspections by 60%. As a result, the client successfully passed multiple federal environmental audits.

Mining Operation in Northern Ontario

Enviro Test Solutions worked with a mining company to deploy a ruggedized monitoring system in a remote location. Using solar-powered sensors with LoRaWAN, the system collected 24/7 air quality data, which was streamed to their Toronto headquarters via our secure cloud platform. The deployment met all Environment and Climate Change Canada (ECCC) standards.
